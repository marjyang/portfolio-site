Below are my interactive projects; the following range from immersive installations to web applications and java sketches.

RISING

Collaborative Project: Immersive Game Installation

Rising is an immersive roleplaying experience that aims to educate users about the impact of US carbon emissions on sea level rise - more particularly, the impact of specific actions made by different socio-economic roles. Rising was funded by the NYU Tisch Summer Grant Program, and is now displayed in NYU Bobst Library for the environmental awareness exhibition, *This is Not A Drill*, until Winter of 2022. Alongside, the data research from the project was nominated as a top candidate for the Design for Adaptation Program in Cumulus Detroit, where team members will be presenting in their annual conference this upcoming November.

NOT VERY PEACHY

Immersive VR Game

Logline: Not Very Peachy puts you in the perspective of a peach. Embodying this fruit, you navigate growing in the conditions of intensive agriculture - transporting from a fruit field to a consumer's kitchen.

DIGITAL TIME CAPSULE

Web Application

The Digital Time Capsule allows you to send a message to your future self via email. Simply choose a date, write, and click send!
