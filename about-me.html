I am an aspiring product designer currently completing my BFA in Interactive Media Arts at NYU Tisch, and have previously worked in graphic design and web design/development.

My passion for human-centered design stems from my desire to positively impact people's lives. With technology permeating every aspect of our lives, it is crucial to integrate good design to ensure technology is accessible and usable. I firmly believe that good design plays an integral role in bridging the gap between technology and users.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I enjoy creative outlets like drawing and crafting, exploring nature, and participating in strategic games such as chess and billiards.
