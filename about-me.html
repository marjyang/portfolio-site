Marjorie is an interdisciplinary designer currently completing her B.F.A in Interactive Media Arts at NYU Tisch with double minors in Computer Science and Environmental Studies.

Marjorie has taken up leadership roles and work opportunities in graphic design, marketing, and web design/development, where she learned the fundamentals of consumer behavior and user experience. Within college, she discovered her love for storytelling and human-centered design approaches, and as she prepares to graduate in a year, she hopes to continue to apply her technical skills and love of problem-solving beyond her coursework.

Beyond design, Marjorie enjoys exploring parks, playing chess and billiards, drawing, and her evening yoga routines.
