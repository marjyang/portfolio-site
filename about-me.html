Marjorie is an interdisciplinary designer currently completing her B.F.A in Interactive Media Arts at NYU Tisch with double minors in Computer Science and Environmental Studies.

Throughout secondary school to her college career, she took up leadership roles and internship opportunities in graphic design and marketing, where she learned the fundamentals of consumer behavior and user experience. After a few college coding courses, she discovered her love for web development and creative coding using various coding frameworks. Working in web design and development for her university now, she hopes to continue to apply her technical skills and love of problem-solving.

In her free time, Marjorie enjoys exploring cityscapes and parks, playing chess and billiards, doing yoga, and drawing.
