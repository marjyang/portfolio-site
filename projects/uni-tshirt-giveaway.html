T-SHIRT GIVEAWAY APP
An app to facilitate NYU's annual free t-shirt giveaway for incoming students.
Design & Prototyping Exercise
Software: Figma
Time frame: 3 weeks (2023)
Problem
I remember in my first year, I felt completely unfamiliar with the campus, and NYU's free t-shirt giveaway was my very first on-campus event. Pre-pickup, I had no idea what the designs were. During the pickup, I felt flustered when faced with an overcomplicated process of having to 1) download Eventbrite, 2) register, 3) fill out forms/confirm email, and lastly, 4) show my QR code. As an international student just arriving in the US, clueless about mainstream American apps, I felt confused and alienated.
Goal
To build an app to facilitate NYU's free t-shirt giveaway for incoming students, and to reduce the complicated process of downloading Eventbrite, registering, filling out forms. This app aims to make newcomers, especially international students, feel welcome and less alienated.
